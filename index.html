Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Transparent answers about safety, privacy, and how My Health Companion works for your family.

Daily Check-In is a safety feature where you confirm you're okay at scheduled times (7 AM, 12 PM, 7 PM). Tap the beating heart on your dashboard. If multiple check-ins are missed, nominated guardians are automatically alerted with your location and medical vitals.

When you nominate a guardian, they receive a secure invitation. They have a one-hour window to reject the nomination. If they don't, they are automatically accepted into your network. This ensures you're never without a primary emergency contact during setup.

Yes. Guardian access is strictly limited to emergency-relevant data (blood type, allergies, conditions). Sensitive personal documents and chronic health metrics are hidden and only shared if you specifically grant "Vault Access" to a nominee.

Using rPPG technology, our AI estimates Heart Rate (BPM), Respiratory Rate, and HRV from a simple 30-second video of your face. No wearable hardware is required for these basic vitals.

The score (0-100) analyzes your vitals against standard medical reference ranges. It incorporates Blood Pressure, SpO2, Heart Rate, and activity levels to give you a daily "Snapshot" of your overall well-being.
